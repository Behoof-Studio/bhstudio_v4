Melanie Wilson Photographic

Melanie is a professional photographer located in the lovely (and rainy) Cumbria. She photographs weddings across the North of the United Kingdom. As she says herself – she loves photographing weddings, capturing every little detail of a day that has been thoughtfully chosen by you!
